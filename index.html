HOW TO PLAY

  • A timer will be set for 60 seconds and your first question will be displayed
  • A set of answers will be displayed, please select one
  • If the answer is correct your will receive a 5 second bonus
  • If your answer is wrong you will received a -10 penalty
  • Once all questions have been answered, your score's base on the time remaining
